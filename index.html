Devices

Indie band from the Philippines

Weaving together the new wave sound of the 80s, the brash in-your-face post-punk style of the 2000s,
and the creative freedom of modern indie, Devices mixes decades of inspiration into an eclectic synth-pop sound
driven by the intensity of heart and emotion. Originally intended as an unnamed side project by lead guitarist Arik Gatchalian
and bassist Jacob Ferrer in 2015, the pair noticed promise and quickly expanded the lineup.
The pair then recruited Enzo Alampay for the drums, Andrea Ramos for the synths, and frontman Austin Tan for vocals,
and later on, rhythm guitar. The quintet released their first single, Flowers, in 2016 with two more successive single releases,
until the debut of their first EP - The Element of Surprise, in 2018.
